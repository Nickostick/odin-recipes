Pizza

This pepperoni pizza is out of this world! Make it in 5 minutes flat!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Jar of Pizza Sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Shredded Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 450 Degrees.
  2. Roll out pizza dough and evenly distribute sauce on top of dough.
  3. Add pepperoni and shredded cheese to liking.
  4. Throw in the oven.
  5. Enjoy!
