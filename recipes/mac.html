Mac n Cheese

Instant Pot Mac and Cheese - This macaroni and cheese is every family's favorite comfort food pasta recipe made easy in the Instant Pot! You can have creamy, mouthwatering, homemade mac and cheese for dinner in about 10 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Elbow Noodles
  • Velveeta Cheese Square
  • Shredded Cheese

Steps

  1. Put noodles and velveeta cheese in crock pot.
  2. Heat on high for 4 hours.
  3. Stir occasionally.
  4. Turn off crockpot and sprinkle some shredded cheese on top.
  5. Enjoy!
